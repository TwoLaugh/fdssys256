# Additional Ideas — Unsettled / Future

*Items that were previously here but are now part of the core design have been moved to their respective docs. What remains are genuinely unsettled future possibilities.*

## Worth Considering

### Meal Prep Scheduling
Not just what to cook, but when to prep.
- "Marinate chicken tonight for tomorrow's dinner"
- "Batch cook Sunday: make rice, prep veg, make sauce — covers Mon-Wed lunches"
- Timeline view of prep tasks across the week
- Status: could be part of Phase 2 planner, but not yet designed in detail

### Seasonal / On-Sale Awareness
- Integrate with Tesco's current offers page
- Prefer in-season produce (cheaper, tastier)
- "Lamb is on offer this week — want me to plan around it?"
- Status: natural extension of Tesco integration (Phase 3)

### Price History / Smart Buying
- Track prices of commonly bought items over time
- "Chicken breast was £4/kg last month, now £5.50 — buy extra and freeze?"
- Budget forecasting based on planned meals
- Status: requires price data accumulation over time

### Leftover Recipes
- When a meal produces leftovers, suggest how to use them
- "You have leftover rice and chicken — fried rice tomorrow?"
- Status: partially covered by ingredient utilisation planning, but explicit leftover handling could be a nice addition

### Natural Language Search
- "Show me high protein meals I haven't made in a month"
- "What did I eat last Tuesday?"
- AI-powered search across all system data
- Status: high value, could be a core feature rather than an add-on

### Batch Cooking Intelligence
- Shared prep across recipes ("make a big batch of tomato sauce Sunday, use in 3 recipes")
- Identify recipes that share base components
- Status: natural extension of ingredient utilisation optimisation

## Someday / Maybe

### Multi-Store Optimisation
- Compare prices across Tesco, Sainsbury's, Aldi
- Split shopping list across stores for best value
- Only worth it if you actually shop at multiple stores

### Social / Recipe Sharing
- Share recipes with friends/family
- Import from someone else's instance
- Community features if ever goes multi-user

### Meal Rating Predictions
- After enough data, AI predicts how much you'll like a recipe before trying it
- "Based on your preferences, I think you'll rate this 4/5"
- Influences discovery and planning

### Garden / Growing Integration
- Track what's growing in your garden
- "Your tomatoes will be ready in 2 weeks — planning recipes that use fresh tomatoes"

### Carbon Footprint Tracking
- Estimate carbon impact per meal
- Weekly sustainability score
- Carbon budget alongside calorie budget
